  • Pink Floyd

Pink Floyd are an English rock band formed in London in 1965. Gaining an early following as one of the first British psychedelic groups,
they were distinguished by their extended compositions, sonic experimentation, philosophical lyrics and elaborate live shows.
wikipedia

Some of their famous Songs

  1. Another Brick in the Wall (1979)
  2. Money (1973)
  3. Wish You Were Here (1975)
  4. Comfortably Numb (1980)
  5. Time (1973)

  • The Beatles

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960, comprising John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr.
They are regarded as the most influential band of all time and were integral to the development of 1960s counterculture and the recognition
of popular music as an art form. wikipedia

Some of their famous Songs

  1. Let It Be (1970)
  2. Hey Jude (1968)
  3. Come Together (1969)
  4. Here Come The Sun (1969)
  5. Don't Let Me Down (1970)

  • Led Zeppelin

Led Zeppelin were an English rock band formed in London in 1968. The group comprised vocalist Robert Plant, guitarist Jimmy Page, bassist and
keyboardist John Paul Jones, and drummer John Bonham.
wikipedia

Some of their famous Songs

  1. Stairway to Heaven (1971)
  2. Whole Lotta Love (1969)
  3. Immigrant Song (1970)
  4. Kashmir (1975)
  5. Black Dog (1971)
